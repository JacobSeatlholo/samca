Our Mandate

Reimagining mining as a force where measurable impact replaces symbolic commitment.

SAMCA exists to solve one of the mining industry's most persistent challenges: the disconnect between mining operations and the communities that host them.

Progress is reflected in both economic performance and community wellbeing. We provide the frameworks, advisory services, and platforms that enable mining companies to secure their social license to operate, unlock long-term value, and attract global capital through evidence-based impact.

The Summit introduces a complementary and solution-focused platform that prioritises integration, inclusion, and shared economic value within mining communities.

Education Initiative

Mining Integration Summit School Innovation Program

About

The programme invites schools to participate in a structured competition where learners will develop practical, innovative, and community-focused solutions that address social, environmental, economic or technological issues affecting mining communities.

Learn More

Themes for the Competition

Schools are invited to submit solutions addressing real challenges such as:

  • Environmental rehabilitation in mining areas
  • Water and soil quality challenges
  • Youth unemployment and skills development
  • Digital solutions for rural communities
  • Community safety and infrastructure
  • Energy and sustainability
  • Small business and supplier development
  • Health and social challenges in mining towns
  • Technology in mining or environmental monitoring
