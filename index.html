Framework

Our Summit Pillars

Why Attend?

Africa's most resource-rich sector, under-integrated in local participation.

The Mining Integration Summit addresses this gap by creating a platform where Policy Meets Implementation → Capital Meets Opportunity → Innovation Meets Industry Demand.

01

Pillar One

Identifying Needs, Gaps & Opportunities

  • Operational challenges affecting productivity and social licence
  • Supply chain gaps within the mining ecosystem
  • Strategies to improve ESG performance and community engagement
02

Pillar Two

Needs, Participation & Shared Prosperity

  • Local economic development needs
  • Opportunities for community-owned enterprises
  • Skills development priorities
  • Mechanisms to strengthen trust between communities and mining houses
03

Pillar Three

Empowerment & Leadership

  • Increasing women's participation across the mining value chain
  • Expanding procurement for women-owned enterprises
  • Addressing barriers to entry within mining careers
04

Pillar Four

Pathways to Opportunity

  • Connecting youth with training and apprenticeship opportunities
  • Youth-led innovation in mining technologies
  • Investment and mentorship for young entrepreneurs
  • Pathways from education into the mining workforce
05

Pillar Five

Compliance & Policy Through Integration

  • Mining contributes approximately 7–8% of South Africa's GDP
  • The sector supports more than 450,000 direct jobs
  • Mining exports generate over R1 trillion annually
06

Pillar Six

School Innovation Program

Schools participate in a structured competition developing innovative, community-focused solutions.

Mining Integration Summit 2026

Register Now
About the Summit

The first platform of its kind dedicated to strengthening the relationship between mining and society.

Summit Pillar

Sustainable Harmony

Building sustainable harmony through responsible mining, community partnership, environmental care, and economic growth that creates long-term value for people, industry, and future generations.

Summit Pillar

Accountability for Transformation

Driving accountable transformation by creating opportunities for underrepresented demographics through inclusion, skills development, leadership access, and meaningful economic participation.

Summit Pillar

Mayoral Charity Golf Challenge

A Charity Golf Challenge bringing leaders together for networking, partnership building, and giving back — combining sport, philanthropy, and meaningful connections beyond the boardroom.

What to Expect

Why Attend the Summit?

Policy Meets Implementation

High-level dialogue between government officials, mining executives, and community leaders that translates policy into on-the-ground action.

Capital Meets Opportunity

Direct access to investors, financial institutions, and development funding partners actively seeking mining sector opportunities in Africa.

Innovation Meets Industry Demand

Showcase of cutting-edge technologies, youth-led solutions, and sustainable mining practices addressing the sector's most pressing challenges.

Strategic Collaboration

Our Partnership Philosophy

SAMCA Strategic Collaboration and Partnership — We position collaboration and partnerships at the intersection of mining, capital, and community impact, aligning on clear outcomes informed by data-driven insights; we scale what delivers measurable results and leverage our access to connect capability with opportunity ensuring every partnership leaves a tangible, measurable footprint in local value chains and sustainable community development.

Purposeful Alignment

We align partners on clear, measurable outcomes — ensuring every collaboration delivers real impact across the mining value chain.

Data-Driven Insight

Informed by evidence and analytics, we scale what delivers measurable results and discontinue what doesn't.

Community Footprint

Every partnership must leave a tangible, measurable footprint in local value chains and sustainable community development.
